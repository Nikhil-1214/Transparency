Policy Initiative

One Nation, One Election (ONOE)

Synchronization of the Lok Sabha and State Legislative Assembly elections to be held simultaneously. This reform seeks to bring stability and efficiency to the Indian democratic exercise.

Governance Continuity

Eliminates the paralysis caused by the frequent "Model Code of Conduct," allowing for faster policy implementation.

Reduced Public Spending

Estimated to save billions by consolidating logistics, security deployment, and election staff training into a single window.

Impact on Society

Voter Turnout

Higher engagement by reducing frequency and "voter fatigue" across the national landscape.

Social Peace

Shortening campaign periods limits the duration of political friction and polarization.

Public Services

Minimizes the redirection of government officials and teachers from their primary duties.

Federal Balance

Sparks healthy debate on how national and state narratives will coexist in one poll.
